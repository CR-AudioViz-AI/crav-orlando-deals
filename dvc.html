Frequently Asked Questions

Is DVC rental legal and safe?

Yes! DVC owners are allowed to rent their points. Established brokers like David's and DVC Rental Store have facilitated thousands of rentals. They provide insurance and guarantees.

What's the catch?

DVC rentals are typically non-refundable and non-changeable. You need to commit to your dates. Book through a reputable broker with good reviews.

How far in advance should I book?

Popular dates (holidays, spring break) should be booked 7-11 months ahead. Off-season dates may be available 2-3 months out. "Confirmed" rentals are already booked and available immediately.

Do I get the same room as if I booked direct?

Yes! Same rooms, same amenities, same check-in process. You'll check in as a DVC guest and get all resort benefits including Early Entry and Extended Evening Hours.
