üèÉ Beat the Crowds Like a Local

The Rope Drop Trick

Arrive 45 mins before posted opening. They often let guests into the park 15-30 mins early. You can walk to rides and be first in line.

The Fireworks Hack

While everyone watches fireworks, ride the popular attractions with 50-70% shorter lines. Space Mountain has 10-min waits during Happily Ever After.

The Rain Strategy

Florida afternoon storms last 20-40 mins. Hide in a show or restaurant. When rain stops, crowds leave but YOU stay. Enjoy walk-on rides!

The Left Turn Rule

Most people instinctively turn right when entering a park. Go LEFT to find shorter lines throughout the day.

The Last Hour Rush

Stay until park close. Last 60 minutes have the shortest waits of the day as tired families leave.
